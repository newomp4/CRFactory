Welcome to CRFactory

Pull a YouTube channel's top shorts, stitch your CTA on the end, and drop the finished mp4s into a folder ready for upload.

  1. Create a project (one per niche or campaign)
  2. Add channels and upload your CTA video
  3. Hit Scrape to pull top shorts metadata
  4. Hit Process to download, stitch, and export
